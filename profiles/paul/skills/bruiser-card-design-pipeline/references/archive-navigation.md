# Archive Navigation — Finding Lost Card Data

When the current Bruiser canon (`Final_Name_Pass_*.md`, `Roster_33.md`) shows cards with `?/?` stats or bare concepts ("needs workup," "safeguard body"), the full scored proposals exist in one of three places:

## Search Order

1. **Session Context** — `docs/Paul/Brain/Session Context/`  
   The pre-Final Name Pass cascade roster (`*Cascade_Pass_Roster*.md`) has the original 33-card list with ALL stats and rarities — before cuts and renames. This is the fastest recovery path.

2. **Archived Craft Passes** — `docs/archive/workspace_merged_*/batch2/`  
   The `Bruiser_[N]V_Craft_Pass_*.md` files have full frozen-sheet proposals with ranked variants, body/ability Δ, and scorer verdicts. These are agent proposals, not ship canon, but the numbers are scored and verified.

3. **Card Bank** — `docs/Five_Crests/factions/Bruiser/Card_Bank.md`  
   Cuts from Final Name Pass and cascade test salvage. Cards here have stats from their source passes.

## Why This Happens

The Final Name Pass applied cuts and brought in new concepts from Joe during lock-pass sessions. Cards that were "named in flow" got placeholder entries but never received the full scoring treatment the cascade roster cards had. The archive holds the original work.

## Pitfall

Craft passes in the workspace (`docs/Paul/workspace/bruiser_revisions/Bruiser_*V_Craft_Pass_*.md`) are now **stubs** — they point to the archive. Read the archive originals, not the stubs.
