# Body Composition Plan — Template

Progressive, one-habit-at-a-time approach for Joe. He explicitly said all-at-once (meal prep + macros + workouts) makes him fall apart.

## Phase structure

| Phase | Duration | Single Habit | Success Metric |
|-------|----------|-------------|----------------|
| **Phase 1: Track** | 2 weeks | Log everything you eat. No calorie targets, no food rules. | 14/14 days logged |
| **Phase 2: Protein First** | 2 weeks | Hit 160g protein/day. Everything else unchanged. | ≥150g on 12/14 days |
| **Phase 3: Calorie Ceiling** | 2 weeks | Cap at 2,600–2,700 kcal/day. Protein still 160g. | 12/14 days under ceiling |
| **Phase 4: Full Macros** | Ongoing | P: 160g, F: 65–75g, C: remainder (~320–345g) | 80% compliance sustained |

## Baseline metrics to capture

- Weight (kg)
- Est. body fat %
- Fat mass (kg)
- Lean body mass (kg)
- Maintenance calories (est. from BMR × activity factor)
- Age, height

## Target milestones (adjust weight values per individual)

Use ~0.5 kg/week as sustainable rate. Build milestone table:
- Start weight
- -5% weight
- -10% weight  
- Major goal weight (e.g., double digits, target BF%)

## Deliverables

Create plan file at `workouts/body-comp-plan.md` containing:
1. Baseline metrics table
2. Target timeline with milestones and estimated dates
3. Phase-by-phase breakdown (one habit per phase)
4. Weight log table (date, weight, delta, notes)
5. Key principles section

## Key principles to include

1. **One habit at a time.** Master one before adding the next.
2. **Perfect is the enemy of good.** 80% compliance beats 100% for two weeks then quitting.
3. **Training stays constant.** Don't add cardio or change the program during a cut.
4. **Weekends count.** Most people undo their weekday deficit on Saturday/Sunday.
5. **Weigh daily, judge weekly.** Daily fluctuations are water/sodium; weekly average is signal.

## App recommendations

- **Cronometer** — free, tracks micronutrients
- **MacroFactor** — paid, auto-adjusts calorie targets based on real weight data (worth it for serious tracking)
