## Core Program Structure

**Frequency:** 3 non-consecutive days/week (e.g., Mon/Wed/Fri)  
**Duration:** 45–75 min per session  

### The 6 Core Movements (every session)

| # | Movement Pattern | Exercise Options |
|---|-----------------|------------------|
| 1 | Quad-dominant lower | Barbell Squat, Hack Squat, Leg Press, Goblet Squat |
| 2 | Hip-dominant lower | Romanian Deadlift, Trap-Bar DL, Seated Leg Curl |
| 3 | Horizontal push | Barbell/DB Bench Press, Machine Press |
| 4 | Horizontal pull | DB Row, Seated Cable Row, Seal Row |
| 5 | Vertical push | DB/Barbell OHP, Machine Shoulder Press |
| 6 | Vertical pull | Lat Pulldown, Pull-Ups/Chin-Ups |

### Programming

- **Sets:** 3 per exercise
- **Reps:** 8–15 (compounds toward lower end, 6–10 for bench)
- **RIR:** 1–2 reps in reserve (stop before technical failure)
- **Eccentrics:** ~3 second lowering phase
- **Rest:** 2–3 min compounds, 1–2 min accessories
- **Progression:** Add weight/reps/technique quality weekly
- **Deload:** Every 6–8 weeks, reduce volume/intensity 20–40%

### Optional Add-Ons (1–2 sessions/week)

- Lateral raises or rear delt work — 2×12–15
- Biceps/triceps superset — 1–2×10–15
- Calves — 2×10–15
- Core — 2–3 sets (hanging leg raises, ab wheel, cable crunches)

## Key Principles

- **Form over ego:** Full ROM, controlled reps, proper bracing
- **Individualization:** Swap variations based on body structure, mobility, injury history
- **Recovery:** Sleep 7–9h, protein 1.6–2.2g/kg, slight surplus for growth
- **Tracking:** Log workouts, progressive overload, deload when needed

## Ethier's Style

Science-based, evidence-backed. Emphasizes efficiency — "minimum effective dose" approach. Heavy use of compound movements. Personalization is a core theme — he stresses choosing variations that fit YOUR body, not just copying a template.
