# Bouncer Design Walk-Through

**Date:** 2026-05-27
**Purpose:** Complete example of the card design process — from concept to locked card. Joe designated this as a teaching reference.

**Keyword ecosystem (locked):**
- Primary: Vendetta, Grit, Rampage
- Secondary: Hobble (combat damage → kneecap)
- Universals: Overkill, Shell, Hustle, Pizzo

---

## Step 1: What gap needs filling?

2V Spread slot. Gopher (1/1, Hustle, BC: Draw 1) already covers card advantage. Spread at 2V shouldn't duplicate Hall's reliable draw. Need something different.

Spread flexes — muscle, disruption, gambles, underbelly. Not card advantage.

## Step 2: Concept

**Paul's initial pitch:** Bouncer at the social club door. BC: "Reveal top card of opponent's deck. If it's a minion, discard it." He checks IDs, turns people away.

Joe's reaction: Like the idea. Spread muscle. Underbelly, not card advantage. But the card feels clinical — "Bouncer checks list, discards a card" reads like a game mechanic, not a scene.

## Step 3: Flavor upgrade

Joe: "If you wanted to add additional flavor — if it's a minion, he gains +1 ATK."

Now the scene is visible: bouncer checks the list, finds a name that shouldn't be there, grabs the guy by the collar and tosses him into the alley. The +1 ATK is temporary (this turn only) — he goes right back to checking IDs. The moment passes.

**Paul's call:** Version 2 with the kicker. "Without it, clinical. With it, you can SEE the scene."

## Step 4: Scoring variants

2V vanilla budget: 4 pts. Ability estimate: ~0.9 (reveal top, discard minion). Kicker (+1 ATK this turn) adds ~0.3.

| Body | Body Δ | Total Δ | Verdict |
|------|--------|----------|---------|
| 2/2 | 0.0 | **+1.2** | Clean. Strong side but not extreme. Temporary +1 ATK is modest. |
| 2/3 | +1.0 | **+2.2** | ✗ Over ceiling. 2/3 Common with disruption and buff is too much. |

2/2 at +1.2 is the sweet spot. He's not the head of security — he's the guy at the social club door. Imposing but not immovable.

## Step 5: Cross-card synergy

Front Desk (2V Hall, BC: Scry 2) scrys the top card → Bouncer checks the scried card. She sorts the information, he enforces it. No keyword tie — just two people in the same organization doing their jobs.

## Step 6: Locked

**Bouncer** — 2V C, Spread. 2/2.
BC: Reveal top card of opponent's deck. If it's a minion, discard it and this gains +1 ATK this turn.

---

## Process Lessons

1. **Start from "what gap needs filling"** — not "what abilities are available."
2. **Concept → flavor upgrade → scoring variants → lock.** Don't skip from concept to lock.
3. **Present body variants with deltas.** Let the numbers inform the decision, not lead it.
4. **Check cross-card synergy.** Even without keyword ties, cards can talk to each other.
5. **Spread flexes.** This card is muscle/disruption. It is NOT card advantage. That's Hall's job.
