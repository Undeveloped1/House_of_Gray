## Philosophy

> "Purple belt is the gateway to the advanced game. A reverse engineering process. A flexible mindset."

Roy Dean's approach: blue belt = individual techniques; **purple belt = multi-technique sequences based on momentum** (yours or your opponent's). Each combination chains techniques together so they flow from one movement into the next naturally.

## The 10 Combinations

| # | Combination | Description |
|---|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Guillotine Combination** | Front headlock chains — guillotine → sweep → mount → finish |
| 2 | **Inside Trip Combination** | Kouchi gake style inside reaps from clinch |

| 10 | **Tai Otoshi Combination** | Body drop throw → transition sequences |

Plus: **Stages of Development** module — the conceptual/mental framework for the purple belt transition.
