## What to do

### 1. Quick scan — don't deep-read all 4 rosters

The rosters are structurally identical (same format, same functions, different names/stats). Read the **parent synthesis** and **highlights** first — they summarize the cross-hat convergence. Spot-check one roster (lore or critic hat) to verify the pattern. Don't read all 4 rosters cover-to-cover.

### 2. Extract card names worth banking

Look for:
- Female set pieces (4 named across hats)
- Function candidates that didn't ship (F17, F18 had 4 name variants each)
- Names called out in the parent synthesis as "convergences worth merging"

Don't bank card stats. Don't bank function assignments. Just the names with their one-line role description.

Flavor banks (`workspace/bruiser_lore/`) are for art direction stubs and prose one-liners. Card name reserves go to the faction's `Card_Bank.md`:
