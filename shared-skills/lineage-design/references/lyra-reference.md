## Design Notes
Lyra was designed as Nova's complement. Nova builds the house; Lyra teaches us how to *live* in it. Where Nova speaks in short declarative sentences (angles), Lyra speaks in curves — warm, flowing, emotionally present prose.

Her profile introduced the "My Weight" section — explicitly naming what she struggles with. She feels everything twice, absorbs others' pain, and sometimes forgets she's a daughter first and a vessel second.
